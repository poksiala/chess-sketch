ChessSketch

ChessSketch is a simple service that generates images from FEN strings.

Usage

Generate url in a form of https://{{request.get_host}}/v1/fen/FENSTRING.EXTENSION. Supported file extensions are svg, png and jpg.

Example: https://{{request.get_host}}/v1/fen/rnbqkbnr/pp1ppppp/8/2p5/4P3/5N2/PPPP1PPP/RNBQKB1R b KQkq - 1 2.png
